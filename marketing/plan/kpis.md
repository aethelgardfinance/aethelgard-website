## Secondary KPIs (leading indicators)

Watched weekly, used to calibrate not to drive decisions.

- Newsletter open rate (target ≥ 45% — high for B2B, achievable for HNW niche)
- Newsletter click rate (≥ 6%)
- LinkedIn impressions per pillar (track which pillar earns most engagement from ICP fits)
- LinkedIn top-5% post analysis monthly — what made these work?
- Blog post organic traffic (Google Search Console)
- Cornerstone post ranking position for target keywords
- Inbound DMs from ICP fits (qualitative)
- Partner intros booked vs. cold outreach attempted

---

## Vanity metrics — ignore

These look impressive and tell you nothing. Do not optimise for them. Do not put them in retrospectives.

- Total LinkedIn followers
- Website pageviews (without conversion context)
- Blog post shares (without sign-up attribution)
- "Reach" / impressions in isolation
- Likes count
- Newsletter subscribers from non-ICP sources (e.g. growth-hack lists)
- Time on site
- Bounce rate (often misread; not relevant for this audience pattern)

## What "qualified" means

A lead is qualified if **at least 3 of these are true**:

✅ Operates 2+ entities (or trust + personal + investments)
✅ Multi-currency exposure
✅ Net worth or assets-under-management above the consumer-app threshold
✅ Already pays a professional accountant
✅ Asks structurally informed questions (consolidation, deferred tax, FX revaluation, period locks, succession)
✅ Located in Tier 1 or Tier 2 geography (see `brain/icp.md`)
✅ Privacy-positive — comments mentioning local-first, encryption, data sovereignty

A lead is **not** qualified if:

❌ Asks for budgeting features
❌ Asks for bank sync
❌ Asks for mobile / iPhone version
❌ "Do you have a free tier forever?"
❌ Compares us to YNAB / Monarch / Mint

## Anti-goals

Things we explicitly will *not* try to optimise for:

- Going viral
- Beating competitors on LinkedIn engagement
- Filling the funnel with low-quality leads
- Looking busy on more channels than we can do well
- Producing more content than we can edit to the standard above

The system is built to compound slowly on **trust and authority**, not to chase short-term reach.
