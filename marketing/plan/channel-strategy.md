Narrow on purpose. Anything not on the **yes** list is a distraction tax until proven otherwise.

## вң… YES вҖ” primary channels

### LinkedIn (Cherie's personal profile)
- **Primary acquisition + authority channel**
- 3 posts/week (Tue/Wed/Thu morning UK time)
- Founder-led; the *Aethelgard* company page exists as a back-stop, not the lead
- Founder-led posts get ~10Г— the reach of company-page posts on LinkedIn вҖ” this is structural, not preference
- Cadence enforced by the four-week pillar rotation (`plan/content-pillars.md`)

### Owned newsletter (Beehiiv)
- **Primary trust-building channel**
- Monthly long-form, 1500вҖ“3000 words
- Free; no paid tier вҖ” the product is the conversion event
- Sign-up embedded on every page of the website
- Higher signal, lower noise than LinkedIn вҖ” much better conversion to paying customer
- Why Beehiiv: free to 2,500 subs, owns the data, better deliverability than Substack

## вң… YES вҖ” secondary channels

### Website blog (`aethelgard.finance/blog`)
- **SEO + credibility engine**
- 2000вҖ“3500 words per post; biweekly cadence (quality over volume)
- Cornerstone topics вҖ” see `plan/backlog-50.md` once written
- Each post recycled into 3вҖ“5 LinkedIn posts + 1 newsletter section + 1 carousel
- Every post links to the trial download

### Strategic partnerships
- **Highest-ROI compounding channel; slowest to build**
- 5 hand-picked allies in year 1
- Trade: free Aethelgard Lifetime + co-authored content, in exchange for warm intros and case-study material
- Targets:
  - 2вҖ“3 Koninklijke NBA / ICAEW practitioners serving HNW
  - 1вҖ“2 boutique IFAs / wealth advisors
  - 1 STEP / trust practitioner
  - 1 specialist tax solicitor
- Logged in `network/outreach-log.md`

### Podcast guest appearances
- 1 per quarter target
- Targets: Family Office Podcast, The Family Business Podcast, Making Money Make Sense, MoneyWeek, BeursTalk (NL), founder-finance podcasts where the host has exited
- Pre-empt with a 1-page media kit + 5 angle ideas the host can pick from
- **Do not start a podcast.** Be on others.

- Always co-promotes the newsletter

## вқҢ NO вҖ” explicit rejects

### Facebook
- **Wrong demographic.** HNW founders, family-office principals, and accountants do not research professional finance tools on Facebook.
- Organic reach is dead.
- Aethelgard on Facebook actively hurts the premium positioning.
- Skip organic FB entirely.
- Possible exception: paid lookalike off existing buyer list, *only* once buyer list вүҘ 50.

### Twitter / X
- Low signal-to-noise for this audience.
- Niche fintwit isn't our ICP.
- The cost of doing it well exceeds the upside.

### TikTok / Instagram (organic)
- Wrong audience entirely.
- "Authentic founder content" on these platforms cannibalises the gravitas the brand depends on.
- No.

### YouTube (founder channel)
- Distracts from writing without proportional reward at our scale.
- Reconsider in year 2 if the audience demands it.

### Mass-email outbound to HNW lists
- Brand-damaging. Spam-positioned.
- Even if technically legal, fundamentally wrong for the audience.

### Affiliate / referral programmes
- Skip in year 1. Risk of brand-misaligned promoters.
