# Aethelgard PDF Studio — Product Brain

The companion product. Same privacy model, different job.

---

## What it is

A **finance-grade PDF editor** built for the documents wealth professionals actually need: trustee resolutions, gift letters, intra-family loan agreements, board minutes, ISA/SIPP transfers, redacted bank statements, signed engagement letters.

Windows desktop, local-first, no Adobe required.

---

## Why it exists

Adobe Acrobat charges by the month for things accountants and family-office principals do once a quarter. Most users don't need a full Acrobat subscription — they need to redact a sort code, place a signature, generate a tidy gift letter for HMRC, and move on.

PDF Studio is built for that. Small, fast, branded, and gone in three clicks.

---

## What it actually does

- **True redaction** — drag a rectangle, page is rasterised; text, metadata and search are gone (not just a black box on top)
- **Signing** — draw with trackpad or upload a transparent PNG
- **HNW templates** — six built-in:
  1. Letter / memo
  2. Gift letter (Declaration of Gift) — HMRC, mortgage providers, lawyers
  3. Trustee resolution
  4. Loan agreement (intra-family)
  5. Board minutes
  6. ISA / SIPP transfer request
- **Custom templates** — placeholder fields, save locally, generate from gallery
- **Multi-entity branding** — separate brand profiles per company/trust/holdco; letterhead, watermarks, footers in one click
- **Form filling that flattens** — AcroForm fields filled and flattened so the recipient sees fixed text
- **Local-first** — no cloud, no telemetry, no upload

## The strategic role of PDF Studio

PDF Studio is **not** a separate revenue play. Its job is:

1. **Top of funnel** — £99 is a low-friction trial of the Aethelgard worldview (local-first, finance-grade, designed for HNW work). Many users will upgrade to Aethelgard Lifetime once they trust the brand.
2. **Partner gift** — for the 5 strategic partners (Koninklijke NBA peers, STEP trustees, HNW IFAs), free PDF Studio licences are a frictionless way to put the brand in front of their clients without asking them to migrate accounting software.
3. **SEO anchor** — "PDF redaction without Adobe", "gift letter template HMRC", "trustee resolution template" are all searchable, ICP-relevant queries that pull warm traffic into the Aethelgard ecosystem.

## What we don't say

- No e-signature legal claims (it's not DocuSign — it places signatures, doesn't certify them)
- No "AI document generation" claims — templates are structured forms, not LLM output
- No mobile (desktop only, by design)
- No team / multi-user features
