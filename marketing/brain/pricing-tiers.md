## Pricing philosophy

- **We are not cheap.** We are not trying to be. The audience values correctness over cost.
- **Lifetime exists** because the ICP includes people who view subscriptions as a vendor risk. Lifetime removes that objection.
- **No freemium of Aethelgard core.** The 30-day free trial is the freemium. After that, the wealth ledger is paid.
- **PDF Studio is annual-only standalone** because monthly billing on a £99 product creates more support load than it earns.
- **Currency:** GBP primary. Listed in GBP on the site.

---

## The funnel logic

```
PDF Studio £99/year  ─┐
                      ├─→ Aethelgard Lifetime (one payment, both products forever)
Aethelgard Personal  ─┘
       ↓
Aethelgard Sovereign or Corporate
       ↓
Aethelgard Lifetime
```

Most paying users will land on either Sovereign or Corporate. The Lifetime upgrade is the long-tail revenue lift driven by trust accrued over time.

## What we say about pricing in content

- **Anchor against the gap.** Not "£X is good value" — but "the alternative is £50K–£200K/year for an institutional platform you can't operate yourself, or a free spreadsheet that doesn't answer the question."
- **Lead with the trial.** 30 days, no account, no credit card, full Corporate features.
- **Lifetime is the quiet recommendation.** Don't push it; let the right buyer find it.
- **Never apologise** for not having a £5/month tier. That's the wrong frame for this audience.
